## Timing Rules
- Wait 3-5 days between follow-ups
- Maximum 3 follow-ups per prospect
- If no response after 3, mark as "cold" and move on
- If response is "not interested", thank them and remove from active list
- If response is positive, move to "warm" status and schedule call
